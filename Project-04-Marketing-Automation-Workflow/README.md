# Marketing Automation Workflow System

## Project Overview
This project demonstrates how a growing skincare ecommerce company can automate its lead management process using CRM and workflow automation tools.

The solution was designed to reduce manual lead handling, improve communication between marketing and sales teams, and create a centralized system for capturing, organizing, and tracking customer leads from multiple marketing channels.

The project combines CRM configuration, workflow automation, marketing operations, and reporting into one business solution.
## Business Context
GlowSkin Labs is a fictional direct-to-consumer (D2C) skincare startup that sells skincare products through its ecommerce website.

Its flagship product is the **GlowSkin Vitamin C Brightening Serum**, supported by additional products such as a hydrating moisturizer, gentle face cleanser, and SPF 50 sunscreen.

The company generates leads through multiple digital marketing channels, including Instagram Ads, Facebook Ads, Google Search, and website forms. As marketing campaigns scale, the existing manual lead management process becomes inefficient and difficult to maintain.
## Business Problem
Marketing campaigns successfully generate new customer leads, but the lead management process relies heavily on manual work.

Marketing team members manually transfer lead information into spreadsheets, sales representatives are informed through manual messages, and follow-up emails are handled individually. This creates several operational challenges:

- Slow response times for new leads
- Duplicate or inconsistent customer records
- Lack of centralized lead management
- Limited visibility into lead status
- Increased administrative effort for marketing and sales teams

The objective of this project is to design and implement an automated lead management workflow that improves operational efficiency while supporting faster customer engagement.
## Project Objectives
The primary objectives of this project are:

- Capture marketing leads from multiple acquisition channels
- Store lead information in a centralized CRM
- Automate repetitive marketing operations tasks
- Notify the sales team immediately when new leads are created
- Standardize lead information across systems
- Improve collaboration between marketing and sales
- Create an operational reporting dashboard to monitor lead performance

## Solution Overview
The proposed solution replaces a manual lead management process with an automated workflow that connects lead capture, CRM management, team notifications, and reporting.

New marketing leads are first captured in a structured database, automatically synchronized with the CRM, and shared with the sales team through workflow automation. This reduces repetitive manual tasks while improving lead visibility and response time.

The solution was designed using free or community versions of industry-standard marketing operations tools to demonstrate a practical and scalable workflow.
